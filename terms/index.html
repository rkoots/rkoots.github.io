Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. At RKoots, our objective is to provide educational content for learning purposes, and we value the trust you place in us. Please read this Privacy Policy to understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

This website is intended solely for educational purposes, with the content curated from publicly available information and social learning platforms. We do not claim responsibility for the accuracy or completeness of the information provided on the website. All content is provided in good faith to foster learning, not for commercial or misleading purposes.

Log Files

Like many websites, RKoots collects standard log files that track user activity on the site. These logs include:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser types
  • Internet Service Providers (ISP)
  • Time and date of access
  • Pages viewed
  • Clicks

This information is not linked to personally identifiable data and is used to analyze trends and improve site functionality.

Cookies and Web Beacons

We may use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small data files stored on your browser, helping us to improve site usability. However, you can control cookie settings via your browser preferences.

Third-party advertisers, such as Google, may also use cookies or web beacons on our site. We do not control these practices and encourage you to review their privacy policies for more information.

Google Analytics

RKoots uses Google Analytics to understand user engagement with our site. Google Analytics tracks visitor behavior through cookies, but it does not identify individual users. You can read more about Google Analytics’ privacy practices here.
