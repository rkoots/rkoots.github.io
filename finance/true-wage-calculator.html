Frequently Asked Questions

What is the "true wage" concept?

Coined by Vicki Robin in "Your Money or Your Life," the true wage concept accounts for all time and money you spend because of your job — commute, work clothes, stress decompression time, work lunches. Your real hourly rate is often 30–50% lower than your quoted salary implies.

How does income tax affect my hourly rate in India?

Under the new tax regime, ₹12 LPA income has an effective tax rate of ~10–12% after the standard deduction. Under the old regime with 80C and HRA deductions, effective rates can be lower. Our calculator applies slab rates automatically for both regimes.

Should I include mental decompression time in my true wage?

For high-stress jobs, yes. If you need 1 hour to decompress daily after work, that's 250+ hours/year that belong to your job — even if you're technically at home. Adding this makes the true wage calculation even more eye-opening.
