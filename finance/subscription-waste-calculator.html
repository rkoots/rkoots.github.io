Frequently Asked Questions

How much do Indians spend on subscriptions annually?

Urban Indians typically spend ₹2,000–₹8,000/month on subscriptions (OTT, apps, gyms, cloud storage, news). With household-sharing and forgotten free trials, 30–40% of this spending often goes to unused services.

What's the best way to audit subscriptions?

Check your bank/credit card statement for the past 3 months and list all recurring debits. Then ask: Did I use this last month? Does it justify its cost? Could a free alternative replace it? Cancel everything you can't answer "yes" to.

Should I share streaming subscriptions to save money?

Yes, where permitted. Family/Premium plans for Netflix, Spotify, and YouTube Premium allow multiple profiles at 2–3× the base price — meaning each member pays 50–70% less. Use this for services you actively use and share legitimately.
