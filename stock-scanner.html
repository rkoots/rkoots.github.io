Criteria Checklist
✓
Strong Pre-Session Catalyst
+2
Earnings beat, regulatory approval, major contract win, or M&A news announced after market close or before open.
✓
Price Near 52-Week High
+2
Stock trading within 5% of its 52-week high — shows strong underlying demand and institutional accumulation.
✓
RSI Between 55–70
+1
RSI in momentum zone — strong but not overbought. Avoid RSI > 80 which signals exhaustion risk.
✓
Above 50-Day & 200-Day MA
+1
Price above both moving averages signals a healthy uptrend. Ideal when 50-DMA is also above 200-DMA (golden cross).
✓
Volume Surge (2x Average)
+1
Recent session volume at least 2× the 20-day average. High volume confirms conviction behind any price move.
✓
Breakout Above Resistance
+1
Stock broke above a key resistance or Pivot R1 level in the last 1–3 sessions with meaningful volume.
✓
Positive FII / DII Activity
+1
Foreign or domestic institutional investors net buying in the sector or the specific stock in recent sessions.
✓
Sector Tailwind Active
+1
Sector index (e.g. Nifty IT, Nifty Bank) showing strong momentum. Rising tides lift individual stocks.
✓
Positive Global Cues
+1
US markets (S&P 500, Nasdaq), SGX Nifty, and Asian markets trending up before NSE open.
✗
Recent Heavy Selling / Promoter Exit
−1 ⚠
Promoters offloading stake or heavy institutional selling in last 2 weeks — gap-ups in these are often traps.
Pre-Market Workflow
01
Check Global Cues
SGX Nifty, Dow Futures, Nasdaq. Takes 5 min every morning.
02
Scan Announcements
NSEIndia.com → Corporate Announcements from last 24 hours.
03
Run Screener
Apply momentum + volume + RSI filters on Screener.in or Trendlyne.
04
Score Each Stock
Use this checklist. Only trade setups scoring 6+ points.
05
Set Levels
Mark entry, stop-loss, and target before the session opens. Never chase.
