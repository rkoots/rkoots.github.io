## What Are Photo Prompts and Why They Matter

Photo prompts are structured text instructions used to guide AI image generation tools such as DALL-E, Midjourney, Leonardo, and Gemini. A well-written prompt reduces randomness and gives you control over subject, visual style, composition, lighting, and mood.

High-quality photo prompts matter because they:
- Improve consistency across generated images
- Reduce wasted generations and retries
- Enable faster creative iteration
- Produce production-ready visuals for marketing and ecommerce

Prompt quality directly impacts efficiency, cost, and output reliability.

## Anatomy of a Best-in-Class Photo Prompt

A high-performing photo prompt follows a clear structure. Skipping any part leads to unpredictable results.

### 1. Subject / Main Focus
Define exactly what must appear in the image.

Example:  
A young woman with braided hair

### 2. Details and Style
Control how the image looks and feels.

Includes:
- lighting style
- texture and material
- mood or emotion
- camera or lens details
- artistic direction

### 3. Composition and Environment
Specify layout and context.

Includes:
- foreground and background
- framing and perspective
- indoor or outdoor setting
- studio or natural environment

### 4. Quality Modifiers
Set expectations for realism and output fidelity.

Examples:
- photorealistic
- ultra-realistic
- high detail
- 4K or 8K resolution
- post-processing cues

## Prompt-Tuning Tips That Actually Work

- **Start with a base template** then adjust: subject > style > environment > quality modifiers.
- **Use camera/lens terms** if you want realism: `f/1.8`, `50mm`, `bokeh`, etc.
- **Add emotion/mood** words if you care about atmosphere: `moody`, `serene`, `tense`, etc.
- **Specify realism level**: `ultra-realistic`, `photorealistic`, `digital painting`, `cinematic still`.
