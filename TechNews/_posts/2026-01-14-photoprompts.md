## What Are Photo Prompts and Why They Matter

Photo prompts are structured text inputs designed to get consistent, high-quality image outputs from AI generators (DALL-E, Midjourney, Leonardo, etc). Good prompts reduce randomness, control style, and help you iterate faster on concepts like composition, lighting, and mood.

## Anatomy of a Best-In-Class Photo Prompt

A strong photo prompt includes:

**1) Subject / Main Focus**  
Clear description of what should be in the image.

**2) Details + Style**  
Lighting, texture, mood, camera/lens details, artistic style.

**3) Composition + Environment**  
Foreground/background, placement, context.

**4) Quality Modifiers**  
Resolution hints, realism vs illustration, post-processing cues.

## Example Prompt Templates (Work-Ready)

**Portrait (realistic)**  
`A portrait of a young woman with braided hair, soft natural window light, shallow depth of field, 85mm lens, ultra-realistic skin textures, neutral background, high detail, sharp focus`

**Landscape (cinematic)**  
`Epic mountain valley at sunrise, dramatic clouds, golden hour, wide-angle view, rich colors, high dynamic range, ultra-sharp details, 8k resolution`

**Product (ecommerce clean)**  
`Minimalist shot of a matte black wireless headphone, white seamless background, studio lighting, crisp shadows, 4k, realistic product photograph`

## Prompt-Tuning Tips That Actually Work

- **Start with a base template** then adjust: subject > style > environment > quality modifiers.
- **Use camera/lens terms** if you want realism: `f/1.8`, `50mm`, `bokeh`, etc.
- **Add emotion/mood** words if you care about atmosphere: `moody`, `serene`, `tense`, etc.
- **Specify realism level**: `ultra-realistic`, `photorealistic`, `digital painting`, `cinematic still`.
