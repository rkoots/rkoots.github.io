## What Makes a Good Home Interior Prompt

A reliable interior prompt always defines:

- **Room type** (living room, bedroom, kitchen, etc.)
- **Design style** (modern, Scandinavian, Japandi, luxury)
- **Layout and furniture placement**
- **Lighting direction and temperature**
- **Materials and textures**
- **Image quality modifiers**
