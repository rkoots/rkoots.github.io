# 🧠 Obsidian - Build Your Second Brain

**Obsidian** is a powerful knowledge management and note-taking application that helps you create and navigate a network of interconnected thoughts and ideas. With its linking capabilities and graph view, Obsidian transforms how you organize and discover knowledge.

---

## ✨ Why Obsidian?

- **Local First** - Your notes stay on your device
- **Linking** - Connect ideas with bidirectional links
- **Graph View** - Visualize your knowledge network
- **Extensible** - 1000+ community plugins
- **Markdown Support** - Work with plain text files
- **Free for Personal Use** - No subscription required

---

## 🎯 Key Features

### 🔗 Linking & Connections
- **Bidirectional links** - [[Note]] creates automatic backlinks
- **Link previews** - See connected content instantly
- **Unlinked mentions** - Discover related notes
- **Tagging system** - Organize with #hashtags
- **Aliases** - Multiple names for the same note

### 🕸️ Knowledge Graph
- **Local graph** - Visualize connections between notes
- **Global graph** - See your entire knowledge network
- **Cluster view** - Identify related topics
- **Interactive exploration** - Click nodes to navigate
- **Filter options** - Focus on specific areas

### 📝 Powerful Editor
- **Markdown support** - Full Markdown with extensions
- **Live preview** - See rendered output as you type
- **Split editing** - Source and preview side-by-side
- **Command palette** - Quick access to all features
- **Vim mode** - For Vim enthusiasts

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Download** Obsidian from [obsidian.md](https://obsidian.md)
2. **Create a vault** - Choose a folder for your notes
3. **Write your first note** - Start with [[Welcome]]
4. **Create links** - Connect related ideas
5. **Explore plugins** - Enhance your workflow

## 💻 Core Concepts

### **Vault Structure**
- **Folders** - Organize notes hierarchically
- **Files** - Individual Markdown notes
- **Attachments** - Images, PDFs, and other files
- **Templates** - Pre-formatted note structures
- **Tags** - Cross-cutting organization

### **Linking System**
- **Internal links** - [[Note Name]] for connections
- **External links** - [Text](URL) for web resources
- **Headers** - # for section links
- **Blocks** - ^ for paragraph references
- **Embeds** - ![[Note]] to include content
