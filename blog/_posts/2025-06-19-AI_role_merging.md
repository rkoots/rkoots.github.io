## Overview

AI isn't just reducing roles. It's merging them. In many teams today, functions like QA are being heavily impacted—not just optimized, but in some cases, completely removed. This isn't new. When ATMs arrived, people predicted bank tellers would disappear. Instead, the role evolved into something broader and more valuable.

## The Evolution of Roles

The same shift is happening now:

• QA → Engineering
• Engineering → Product
• Product → Strategy

Execution is getting automated. Thinking is becoming the differentiator.

## The Hidden Value

QA professionals, for example, bring:
• Deep understanding of failure modes
• Strong edge-case thinking
• Ability to anticipate risks early

These are not just testing skills. They are product and system thinking skills.

## Where Human Value Remains

AI can generate code, tests, even roadmaps. But it cannot navigate context, trade-offs, or human dynamics. That's where the real value is moving.

## The Key Takeaway

📌 The shift is simple:
Don't protect your role. Expand your thinking.
