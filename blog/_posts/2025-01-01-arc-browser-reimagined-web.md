# 🌐 Arc Browser - Reimagining the Web Experience

**Arc Browser** by The Browser Company is a revolutionary web browser that challenges traditional browser design with innovative features like vertical tabs, spaces, split view, and AI-powered capabilities that make web browsing more productive and enjoyable.

---

## ✨ Why Arc Browser?

- **Innovative Interface** - Vertical tabs and sidebar navigation
- **Spaces** - Organize browsing by projects and contexts
- **Split View** - Multiple tabs side-by-side
- **AI Integration** - Built-in AI search and summarization
- **Beautiful Design** - Thoughtful animations and interactions

---

## 🎯 Key Features

### 📋 Vertical Tabs
- **Space-efficient** - More screen real estate for content
- **Better organization** - See more tabs at once
- **Easy navigation** - Scroll through tabs intuitively
- **Grouping** - Organize tabs by project or topic

### 🌍 Spaces
- **Workspaces** - Separate browsing contexts
- **Custom icons** - Personalize each space
- **Quick switching** - Jump between projects instantly
- **Isolated sessions** - Different logins and cookies

### 🔄 Split View
- **Side-by-side tabs** - Compare websites easily
- **Resizable panels** - Adjust layout as needed
- **Multiple layouts** - 2, 3, or 4 panel arrangements
- **Independent scrolling** - Navigate panels separately

### 🤖 AI Features
- **Arc Search** - AI-powered search across tabs
- **ChatGPT integration** - Quick AI assistance
- **Summarization** - Get key points from long articles
- **Instant answers** - AI responses in sidebar
