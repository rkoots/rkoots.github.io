    Tech Trends to Watch in 2025

    • Quantum Computing: Companies like IBM and Google are pushing the envelope on quantum computing, with breakthroughs in algorithms.
    • 5G & Edge Computing: With the rollout of 5G networks, edge computing will take data processing closer to the source, improving latency and performance.
    • Blockchain & Decentralized Apps (dApps): Blockchain continues to disrupt industries beyond cryptocurrencies, including decentralized finance (DeFi), healthcare, and supply chain management.
    • AI-Powered Development: The rise of GPT models and other AI-driven tools is reshaping the way software is developed and tested.
    • Docker: For containerizing applications and ensuring consistency across environments.
    • Kubernetes: An open-source container orchestration tool that automates deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications.
    • Terraform: A tool for building, changing, and versioning infrastructure safely and efficiently.
    • AWS Lambda: A serverless computing service that automatically scales your application in response to incoming traffic.

    Measuring Scalability

    Measuring scalability requires a mix of quantitative metrics and qualitative analysis. Key performance indicators (KPIs) to track include:

    • Response Time – How quickly does the system respond under varying loads?
    • Throughput – The number of requests the system can handle in a given timeframe.
    • Error Rates – Understanding the tolerance to errors and maintaining operational stability.
    • Resource Utilization – How efficiently does the system use resources like CPU, memory, and storage under load?
